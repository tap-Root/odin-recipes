Peanut Butter & Jelly

Because who doesn't love a good time?

Description

An irresistible blend of creamy peanut butter and sweet jelly of your choice.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Directions

  1. Spread peanut butter on two slices of bread.
  2. Spread jelly on top of peanut butter.
  3. Enjoy.
